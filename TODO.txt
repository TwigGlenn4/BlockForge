_ Data driven blocks & items
	_ namespace:(block|item|texture|loot_table|recipe|translation)/path/item_name
	_ Json data, png textures
	_ block
		_ Data driven: texture, loot_table
		_ Static: layer:string(solid, foreground), required_tool_type, hardness(break_time=hardness/tool_speed), luminance, transparency?, on_click_function, on_break_function(break other in multiblock, etc), tick_frequency(int =0:never, 1-99:random_by_percent, >=100:every_tick)(goes into world arrays.)
	_ item
		_ Data driven: texture
		_ Static: item_type(tool|consumable|material|placable|armor), max_stack 
		_ Optional: tool_data{tool_type, tool_speed, max_durability, attack_damage}, on_click_function, block, wearable_data:{armor_rating, temperature_resist, max_durability, effect?}
	_ texture
		_ png format, square
		_ detect resolution?
		_ texture atlas for animated, somehow define animation time.
	_ loot_table
		_ block 
			_ correct_tool:array[item_stack]
			_ wrong_tool:array[item_stack]
		_ entity
			_ weapon:array[{weight:int, items:array[item_stack, item_stack]}]
			_ bare_hand:array[{weight:int, items:array[item_stack, item_stack]}]
			_ natural:array[{weight:int, items:array[item_stack, item_stack]}]
		_ gameplay (fishing, etc)
			_ array[{weight:int, items:array[item_stack, item_stack]}]
	_recipe
		_ workstation
		_ time
		_ ingredients:array[item_stack]
		_ results:array[item_stack]
	_ translation
		_ en_us.json
		_ json array
		_ "namespace:(block|item)/path/item_name":"Item Name"
		_ generate translation array with all translations for set lang, default + modded, overwrite when needed
